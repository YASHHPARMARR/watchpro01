The Rolex Submariner’s robust and functional design swiftly became iconic. With their subtly redesigned Oyster case, distinctive dial with large luminescent hour markers, graduated rotatable Cerachrom bezel and solid link Oyster bracelet, the latest generation Submariner and Submariner Date are in line with the original model launched in 1953.

  • Waterproof to 300 meters (1,000 feet)
  • Unidirectional rotatable bezel
  • Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal
  • Cyclops lens over the date
